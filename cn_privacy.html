CeramicNotifier Privacy Policy

This is a very simple privacy policy, for my simple Glassware, CeramicNotifier. Here are the basics:

  • Anonymous metrics will be collected. This helps to understand usage, and make better decisions about development and features.
  • Collection of email and auth tokens will be strictly to enable functionality within the app.
  • There may be, at some point in the future, a server involved. This will only be used to enable more functionality.
  • This app will be free, there will be no ads.
  • None of your information will be sold to a third-party.
  • None of your information will be given to a third-party for advertisement, or money making purposes.
  • The only third-parties involved will be to enable analytics (Google Analytics for now), crash reports (Crashlytics), and a future hosted database (probably DynamoDB) for a future features.
You may contact me at ejohn _AT_ feigdev.com if you have any issues with this.
